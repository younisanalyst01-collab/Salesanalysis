# 📊 Sales Analysis Project

> An end-to-end sales data analysis project — from raw, messy data to actionable business decisions.

---

## 🗂️ Project Overview

This project covers the full data analysis pipeline: cleaning inconsistent raw data, transforming it with Power Query, building KPIs and Pivot Tables, and delivering an interactive Excel dashboard with strategic insights.

The goal wasn't just to visualize numbers — it was to ask the right questions and uncover patterns that challenge assumptions.

## 💡 Strategic Insights

### 1. Electronics Customers Predominantly Pay in Cash
Electronics buyers prefer in-person, cash-based transactions — a signal of **low digital trust**, not low demand. This represents a **conversion gap** that better UX, return policies, or digital payment incentives could close.

### 2. The Lowest-Ordering Country Relies Heavily on PayPal
Limited banking infrastructure or the absence of localized payment options may be suppressing order volume — not a lack of customer interest. Expanding payment options could unlock latent demand.

### 3. High-Volume Categories Generate Weak Revenue
Clothing and Food drive significant order counts but land in the **$50K–$80K** revenue range. Electronics, Furniture, and Office Supplies collectively generate **$300K–$400K**.

> Volume ≠ Value. Strategy should optimize for revenue contribution, not just order count.
